# User Manual — Parshva Jewellers

This guide walks through how to use the app once it's running (see the Installation
Guide if it isn't running yet).

## 1. Browsing the Catalog

- The homepage shows featured collections, wedding jewelry, and gifting categories.
- Use the top navigation to browse by **material** (Gold, Silver, Diamond, Daily Wear) —
  hover over a material to see its subcategories (earrings, necklaces, rings, etc.).
- Use **Collections** in the navigation for themed groupings (Kundan Stories, Rajwadi
  Heritage, Polki Collection, Festive Collection).
- Use the search bar (top of the page) to search by name, category, or material.

## 2. Trying On a Piece

1. Click any product image, or its "Virtual Try-On" button.
2. Your browser will ask for **camera permission** — allow it.
3. Position yourself in frame. The jewelry will render live on your camera feed.
4. Use the **zoom controls** (+ / −) to adjust the size of the overlay if needed.
5. Check the **AI Stylist panel** on the side — it shows recommended pieces that pair
   well with what you're trying on. Click one to add it to your look (try on multiple
   pieces at once).
6. Click **End Session** to stop the camera when you're done.

## 3. Creating an Account / Signing In

1. Click the profile icon (top right) → **Sign In / Register**.
2. **New users**: switch to "Create Account", enter your name and phone number, submit.
3. **Existing users**: enter your phone number under "Sign In", submit.
4. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code (shown directly in the interface during
   development/testing — in production this would be sent via SMS).
5. Enter the code. You're now signed in.
6. To resend a code, use "Resend OTP" (available after a short cooldown).

## 4. Cart and Wishlist

- Click the **heart icon** on any product to add/remove it from your wishlist.
- Click **Add to Cart** to add a product to your cart.
- Access your cart via the bag icon in the navigation — adjust quantities with +/−, or
  remove items entirely.
- Your wishlist is accessible from the profile dropdown menu.
- Both require you to be signed in.

## 5. Your Profile

- Click the profile icon → **My Profile** to view your account details.
- Sign out via **Sign Out** in the same dropdown menu.

## 6. Admin Panel (admin accounts only)

- Accessible via **Admin Panel** in the profile dropdown, if your account has admin
  permissions.
- View all products, filter by category, edit prices/materials/descriptions, add new
  products with an image upload, or deactivate/delete existing products.

## Troubleshooting

- **Camera doesn't open**: make sure you allowed camera permission in your browser, and
  that no other application is currently using your webcam.
- **"Please sign in first" when adding to cart/wishlist**: you need to be signed in for
  these actions — you'll be redirected to the sign-in page automatically.
- **OTP not working**: request a fresh code with "Resend OTP" — old codes expire after a
  few minutes.
